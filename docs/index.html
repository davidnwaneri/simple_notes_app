If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so directly from within the SimpleNotes app.

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your notes and data will be permanently removed.

To delete your account:

  1. Open the SimpleNotes app on your device
  2. Navigate to your Profile or Settings section
  3. Look for the "Account Settings" or "Delete Account" option
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm account deletion
  5. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted

If you encounter any issues with account deletion or have questions, please contact the app developer through the repository issues page.
